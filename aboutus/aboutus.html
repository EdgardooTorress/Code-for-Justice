About Us

Checkup For All is a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping low-income families. We spread awareness in our local communities about the lack of accessible & affordable healthcare. Checkup For All also serves these underserved communities with the resources they need, be it financial or health literacy. At Checkup For All, we believe healthcare shouldn't be a luxury.

Mission Statement

Making preventive healthcare accessible to low-income and uninsured individuals by providing free or affordable checkups, screening and health education.

What is Checkup For All?

Many low-income households do not have adequate access to annual wellness appointments; That's where Checkup For All comes in. Checkup For All is a non-profit organization who serves low-income households with a guarantee in healthcare check ups. We provide a no-cost annual check ups at any hospital network. Whether uninsured or insured, we also provide financial assistance for further wellness appointments & emergency visits. Checkup For All also hosts events that offer education in healthcare and wellness. Including wellness workshops & fundraising events. We are always accepting volunteers to join us at our wellness workshops and fundraising events!
