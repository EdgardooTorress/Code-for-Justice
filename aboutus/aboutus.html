About Us

CheckUp For All is a nonprofit organization dedicated to ensuring that everyone — regardless of income, background, or insurance status — has access to basic and preventive health checkups.

Mission Statement

Making preventive healthcare accessible to low-income and uninsured individuals by providing free or affordable checkups, screening and health education.

What is Checkup For All?

Many low-income households do not have adequate access to annual wellness appointments; That's where Checkup For All comes in. Checkup For All is a non-profit organization who serves low-income households with a guarantee in healthcare check ups. We provide a no-cost annual check ups at any hospital network. Whether uninsured or insured, we also provide finacial assitance for further wellness appointments & emergency visits. Checkup For All also hosts events that offer education in healthcare and wellness. Including wellness workshops & fundraising events. We are always accepting volunteers to join us at our wellness workshops and fundraising events! At Checkup For All, we believe healthcare shouldn't be a luxury.
